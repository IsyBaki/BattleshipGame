Players

  • There are two players.
  • Each player plays against one opponent.

Object of the Game

  • Sink all 5 enemy ships.
  • The first player to do this wins the game.

Ships

  • Carrier => 5 holes
  • Battleship => 4 holes
  • Cruiser => 3 holes
  • Submarine => 3 holes
  • Destroyer => 2 holes

Prepare for Battle

  • Sit facing your opponent.
  • Your opponent must not see your ocean grid.
  • Place all five ships on your ocean grid.
  • Ships can be horizontal or vertical.
  • Ships must not touch or overlap.
  • Do not move ships after the game starts.

How to Play

  • Players take turns.
  • Call one position (example: D-4).
  • The opponent says “Hit” or “Miss”.

Important Rules

  • One shot per turn.
  • Ships never move.
