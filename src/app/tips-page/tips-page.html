Goal of the Game

Sink all enemy ships before your opponent finds yours. You need strategy, observation, and a little bit of luck.

Basic Strategy

  • Place ships mostly vertically.
  • Leave 1–2 spaces between ships.
  • Sometimes let ships touch without overlapping.
  • Mix planning with randomness.

Attacking

  • Start in the center of the board.
  • Use the checkerboard method.
  • Switch areas after repeated misses.

When You Hit a Ship

  • Focus on the target immediately.
  • Check up, down, left, and right.
  • Keep attacking until the ship is sunk.
  • Then resume searching.

Ship Placement

  • Keep distance between ships.
  • Use deceptive placements.
  • Place some ships vertically.
  • Use a mix of patterns.

Observe Your Opponent

  • Recognize firing patterns.
  • Many players target the center first.
  • Avoid predictable placements.

Additional Tips

  • Change your strategy every game.
  • Use multiple strategies together.
  • Adapt to your opponent’s style.

Fun Strategies

  • Place ships along the edges.
  • Place all ships on one side.
  • Try unusual patterns to surprise opponents.
